TOTP (Two-Factor Authentication)

When enabled, two-factor authentication is available for all users. Individual users can still choose to enroll or skip unless you enforce it via policy. TOTP per-user enrollment is managed under each user's profile.

Multi-Node Support

Enables distribution of game servers across multiple remote nodes. The node health scheduler starts at application startup. Changing this setting requires a restart to take effect.

WebAuthn / Passkeys

Changing the Relying Party ID or Origin will invalidate all existing registered passkey credentials. Users will need to re-register their passkeys.
